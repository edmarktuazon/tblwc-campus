TBLWC Profile

The Bearer of Light and Wisdom Colleges, Inc. is a non-profit, non-stock educational institution which was established in 2003, located at 403 Bee Bien Building, Molino 1, City of Bacoor, Cavite, Philippines.

Founded by the President/Director, Ronaldo Arbas Salvador, he organized the Board of Trustees and completed the requirements to pursue his dream his own academic haven, the then the Bearer of Light and Wisdom Academy. On June 2, 2004, it was granted with Certificate of Occupancy and permission to operate basic education program from NKP to grade 6. In 2009, the name was changed to The Bearer of Light and Wisdom Colleges, Inc. when the High School Level was recognized by the Department of Education.

On August 24, 2004, the Securities and Exchange Commission finally released its certificate approving the operation that includes the Commission of Higher Education assent to college courses taking of the technology Education and Skills Department Authority courses.

Today, we are complete with K-12 curriculum, TESDA courses and BA & Education (Elementary, English/Math Major) College Degrees. In pursuit of making this your school and your better future!

Our Vision

BLWC exist as a Christian school to provide every student
1. a quality education to be dynamic, God-loving and God-fearing, in a secured and supportive environment;
2. elevate a globally literate and technologically advanced climate with Christian values of truth; and
3. develop independent self sufficient adults equipped to be responsible citizens endowed with Christ-like values and character.

Our Mission

BLWC is Christian institution designed to adapt the nature and persistence of quality learning, afforded with the trends and updates around the globe at par with technological flow to develop competence, prudence and integrity in a literate and completed individual in pursuit of excellence in education, and to develop Godly people to serve the community to proclaim God’s plan and provision.

Philosopy

“ To advocate Christian education in pursuit of higher learning and to develop holistic faculties of noble individual.”

ANNOUNCEMENT

Education in BLWC is now back to Face to Face modality. Since we were given a permit to offer F2F, the Administration as well as the faculty and staff have prepared of this learning modality. F2F is of advantage due to the teaching –learning process wherein the teachers made use of the modern strategies, approaches & teachings.

  • Develop technology-enriched instructional materials such as lessons, quizzes, games, videos, and PowerPoint presentations.
  • Build an interactive collaboration platform for student-centered learning as well as teacher-centered learning.
  • Design learning experiences where students create their own outputs, fostering creativity and critical thinking.
