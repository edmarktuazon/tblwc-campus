about us

19 years of experience

In 2004, Mr. Ronaldo Salvador, a Christian Businessman, raised in a Seventh-day Adventist home, crafted to pursue the principle that King Solomon in Proverbs 22:6 had verbalized, "Train up a child in the way he should go for when he is old he will not depart from it. Thus, the Bearer of Light and Wisdom Academy was born during the summer, taking-off with spiritual-enrichment program, Vacation Bible School, to the pre- schoolers and enhancement program for the already- schoolers.
